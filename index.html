Research approach

I am committed to disability and climate justice. Beyond academic achievements or career goals, I am interested in creating relations of reciprocity and respect through research projects. As a scholar drawing on theories from Human-Computer Interaction, Anthropology and Science and Technology Studies I advocate for community-led innovation and research driven by those most impacted by social injustice.wohooYay!
