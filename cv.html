Barbara Nino Carreras / cv

I am a PhD Fellow in the Technologies in Practice research group and the SOS Project at the IT University of Copenhagen. In my research, I draw on theories from the field of science and technology studies, anthropology, and human-computer interaction. My research interests include visual anthropology, critical disability studies, critical participatory design, digital welfare, and multimodal storytelling.
