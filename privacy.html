Barbara Nino Carreras / privacy

Data protection and minimization are important. Servers run on electricity and data about us is often used in creepy and unethical ways. I try to collect as little information as possible about you. Just the minimum to keep the site running.

My practical steps to improve this website

  • I have added a Content Security Policy (CSP) meta tag and "no-referrer" attribute so that your browser does not send the HTTP referer header information when you click on a hyperlink on this website. Big thanks to Pia who sent me a "fail" test she run on webbkoll when she visited my site. This test made me aware of these important tags and attributes.
  • I am happy to make changes to the site if you think it is not built properly. It might take a while, but I will try my best.
  • I will follow guidelines for improving the privacy of my visitors as best practices evolve.
