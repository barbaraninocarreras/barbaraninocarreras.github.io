Barbara Nino Carreras / privacy

I think privacy and data minimization is important because servers run on electricity and we are data-saturated. So, I made this website so that I collect as little information as possible about you. Just the minimum to keep the site running.

How?

Well, I am not an expert programmer but I can minimize cookies running on this site by not embedding media from platforms like YouTube. I also do not run any google analytics. It is lovely you are here, but there is no need for me to quantify your visit.

    Practical steps to improve this site

  1. I have added a Content Security Policy (CSP) meta tag and "no-referrer" attribute so that user's browser do not not send the HTTP referer header information when a user clicks on a hyperlink on this website. Big thanks to Pia T. who sent me a "fail" test she run on webbkoll. This test made me aware of these important tags and attributes.
  2. I am happy to make changes to the site if you think it is not built properly.
  3. I will follow guidelines for improving the privacy of my visitors as guidelines and best practices evolve.
