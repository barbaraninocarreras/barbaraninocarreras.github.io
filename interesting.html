Wait, what is an alt-narrative?

The web allows us to show information in different modalities, through sound, imagery, text, and even haptic interactions! This is beneficial to many, especially disabled people.

Alt-text, or alternative text, is a written description, often in the form of code, that ensures blind, low vision, and neurodivergent folks have equal access to visual information.

Alternative text, also known as alt-text, is a written description that ensures equal access to visual information for people who are blind, have low vision, or are neurodivergent. Alt-text is often seen as an additional and tedious task or a matter of accessibility compliance, which is unfortunate. However, alt-text is a remarkable and poetic medium. Researchers in the field of human-computer interaction have even developed a genre of alt-text known as Alt-Narratives.
