About Barbara Nino Carreras

She is a Ph.d. candidate in the Technologies in Practice research group at the IT University of Copenhagen. Her project explores how individuals from diverse backgrounds assist each other in navigating mandatory digital self-services in Denmark.

Wait, what is an alt-narrative?

The web allows us to show information in different modalities, through sound, imagery, text, and even haptic interactions! This is beneficial to many, especially disabled people.

Alt-text, or alternative text, is a written description, often in the form of code, that ensures blind, low vision, and neurodivergent folks have equal access to visual information.

Alt-text is often perceived as an annoying extra task for many web content managers.This is very sad. Because alt-text is a wonderful poetic medium. Most importantly, it is a genre of writing that respects the diversity of ways in which we access information.

We are going to give you some examples using Barbara's Macbook and VoiceOver (a built-in screen reader for Apple computers) with a synthethic computer voice called Kate.
